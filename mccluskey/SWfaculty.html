    Meet the Faculty

Douglas Rosenau, ED.D

Doug Rosenau is a Licensed Psychologist, Pastor, Marriage & Family Therapist, and Diplomate of the American Board of Sexology (ABS).  A pioneer in Christian sex therapy, Doug is the best-selling author of A Celebration of Sex, co-author of  A Celebration of Sex after 50, and Soul Virgins: Redefining Single Sexuality (April 2006). He is a full clinical member of the Society for Sex Therapy and Research (SSTAR) and he teaches Human Sexuality as an adjunct professor at Reformed Theological Seminary and Psychological Studies Institute (PSI).


Christopher McCluskey, MSW

Christopher is a psychotherapist, life coach, Licensed Clinical Social Worker and Diplomate of the American Board of Sexology (ABS).  He teaches Human Sexuality at Reformed Theological Seminary and specializes in sexual enrichment and sexual addictions. He and his wife Rachel are co-authors of  When Two  Become One: Enhancing Sexual Intimacy in Marriage.


Debra Taylor, M.A.

Debra is a Marriage & Family Therapist certified in sex therapy for the American Association of Sex Educators, Counselors, and Therapists (AASECT).  She has taught Human Sexuality at Fuller Theological Seminary, and is co-author of Secrets of Eve: Understanding the Mystery of Female Sexuality.  Debra specializes in women's issues including hormonal imbalance, infertility, body image, anorgasmia, and desire disorders.

Michael Sytsma, Ph.D

Michael is a Licensed Professional Counselor with a Ph.D in Marriage and Family Therapy from the University of Georgia.  He is an ordained Wesleyan minister, having served on pastoral staff for several years.  He is President of Building Intimate Marriages, Inc and is an adjunct professor of sexuality at Asbury, Reformed Theological Seminary & Psychological Studies Institute.
