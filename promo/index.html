Sexual issues are cited as one of the primary reasons for marital conflict and divorce, yet many pastors and Christian counselors feel inadequately prepared to minister in this sensitive area.  Christopher McCluskey is one of the few Christian psychotherapists certified in sex therapy and is a cofounder of the Institute for Sexual Wholeness in Atlanta, a master's level training program for Christian counselors.

Christopher's powerful new book, coauthored with his wife, Rachel, presents a beautiful and compelling vision of God's intentions for marital intimacy.  It details his innovative model, The Lovemaking Cycle©, which is used in Christian colleges and seminaries throughout the U.S. and Canada.  When Two Become One is a great resource for pastors to use in premarital or marriage counseling.
